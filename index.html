About Brain-O-Math Olympiad

Organized by Doon Heritage School, Siliguri , the Brain-O-Math Olympiad 2025 is an interschool competitive exam in Mathematics and Science that evaluates students' logical reasoning, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. The exam provides a platform for young learners to showcase their academic talent, build confidence, and prepare for higher-level challenges while promoting healthy competition among schools.
